About

These are all areas in the US of A, which hopes to one day join the civilized nations of the 3rd rock. Was a musician some and got to play at Kenney's Castaways and Maxwell's. I feel that I must point out that Ken once flew a MiG for fun, and he also invented regular expressions.

Proud analog native.
