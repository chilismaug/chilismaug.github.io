About

I am an Ohioan. Started out as a Hoosier and spent 12 years in the Garden State. Love NY and NJ but you gotta be rich. These are all areas in the US of A, which hopes to one day join the civilized nations of the 3rd rock. Was a musician some and got to play at swell spots like Kenny's Castaways, City Gardens, Maxwell's and Folk City. While in NJ, as a newlywed and then a parent, I became a working computer geek and thankfully got some Unix exposure at AT&T, where Dennis, Ken and Brian kicked things off.

I feel I must point out that Ken once flew a MiG for fun, and he also is suspected of discovering regular expressions and Golang.

Proud analog native.
